📊 Image & enrichment strategy

Hotels & events
Google Places photos, ratings, and hours are a good fit when we have a fixed venue address.
Car rentals
Skip Google Places — pickup counters and broker listings rarely give useful car photos. Prefer provider logo + trip details from the doc, or no image at all.
What we already have in Coda
Provider, car type, pickup/return dates & times, addresses, and confirmation — enough for a strong card without a photo or price.

Design Pattern Notes:
  • Airbnb Style: Large image on top, ratings prominent, clean modern look
  • Compact Horizontal: Small thumbnail left, info right - great for lists
  • Ticket Style: Perforated edge effect, date/time badges, event-focused
  • Premium: Overlays, price badges, amenity icons, luxury feel
  • Logo Badge: Company logo overlaid on image - professional, recognizable
  • Rental Pass: Boarding-pass feel like flights — provider + conf on top, pick-up/return dates with car connector, times below, vehicle at bottom. No location (same drop-off).
  • Compact Logo Thumb: Horizontal list layout — logo in the 100×100 thumbnail slot instead of a photo
Car rentals: provider logo or no image — not Google Places. No price on cards (already booked). Logos from Wikimedia (Hertz, Enterprise, Avis, Budget).
